How to Play

Discover the mysterious Elden Ring Boss in every match! With each guess, the cell colors change to indicate how close you are.

Green (Correct)

The attribute perfectly matches the secret boss!

Orange / Yellow (Partial)

Only applicable to Region. Indicates that the boss is in the same general region, but the specific location is different.

Red (Incorrect)

The attribute does not match the secret boss.

Directional Indicators (Arrows)

In the Phase and Runes columns:

  • 🔼 The secret boss's value is higher than your guess.
  • 🔽 The secret boss's value is lower than your guess.
